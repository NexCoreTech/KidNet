KidNet Username Guidelines

To help keep KidNet safe and friendly for everyone, here are the rules for usernames on our platform:

Allowed:

  • Fun, friendly, and positive usernames
  • Nicknames or made-up names
  • Names that don’t reveal personal info (like your full real name)

Not Allowed:

  • Bad words, inappropriate, or rude language
  • Names pretending to be another person
  • Anything containing personal addresses, phone numbers, emails, or last names
  • Offensive or violent words
  • Anything breaking the KidNet Terms of Use

Important:

  • Parents must approve usernames before kids can use them
  • Parents can change usernames anytime if needed
  • KidNet admins can remove or rename accounts breaking these rules
