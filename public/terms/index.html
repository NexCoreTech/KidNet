KidNet Terms of Use

Welcome to KidNet — a social space made for kids by a kid. To keep our community safe, fun, and friendly, we ask all users and their parents to follow these simple rules:

1. Be Kind and Respectful

KidNet is a place for positive, safe, and fun conversations. Bullying, rude comments, or inappropriate behavior are not allowed.

2. Keep Personal Info Private

Never share personal info like your last name, address, phone number, school name, or email in your profile, posts, or messages.

3. Username Rules

Your username must follow our Username Guidelines. Parents approve usernames before accounts can go live.

4. Parent Oversight

Parents manage their child's account approvals, usernames, and can remove or rename accounts at any time through the Parent Dashboard.

5. No Bad Language or Inappropriate Content

Anything rude, mean, violent, or inappropriate isn’t allowed anywhere on KidNet. Accounts breaking this rule will be removed.

6. Admin Control

KidNet admins can suspend, rename, or remove accounts that break these terms to keep the community safe for everyone.

7. Premium Features

Some optional premium features are available through the Parent Dashboard and require parent approval and setup.
